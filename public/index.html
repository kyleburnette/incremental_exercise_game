Get Motivated!

Globetrotter is an incremental/idle game... but you still have to get out there and start moving to earn points! While you're moving, you earn points in Globetrotter. If you stop moving, you stop earning points!

Explore Your Surroundings!

Globetrotter offers a more structured exercise mode where you can walk to a chosen destination. When you're done, you'll earn points based on how far you went!

Compete With Your Friends

Using our leaderboards, you can compare your scores to your friends' scores for bragging rights!

Cloud Storage

We worry about saving your progress so you can get out there and walk as much as you can.
